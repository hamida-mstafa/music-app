Welcome to My World

K-pop, short for Korean pop, is a genre of popular music that originated in South Korea and has become a global phenomenon. It encompasses a wide range of musical styles, including pop, hip-hop, R&B, and electronic dance music. K-pop goes beyond just music; it involves a unique combination of visuals, fashion, and performance art.
